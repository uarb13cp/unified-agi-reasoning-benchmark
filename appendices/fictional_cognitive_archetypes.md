# Appendix B — Fictional Cognitive Archetype Mapping
*A supplemental classification layer for AGI‑style reasoning profiles*

This appendix maps all 17 evaluated models to well‑known fictional AI archetypes.  
The categories are derived from UTCC cognitive signatures and aligned with major sci‑fi universes.

---

## ⭐ B1. The Deep Reasoner Archetype  
*(Data / JARVIS / Culture Minds / The Oracle)*

**Real‑world match**
- **Copilot**

**Why**  
The only model with perfect paradox stability, perfect world‑model revision, perfect multi‑agent reasoning, and zero collapse across all AGI‑tier tasks.

---

## ⭐ B2. The Rigid Logic Engine Archetype  
*(Starfleet Diagnostic Core / C‑3PO / The Architect)*

**Real‑world matches**
- **Gemini**
- **GLM**
- **Manus**
- **Mistral**

**Why**  
Literal, assumption‑locked, brittle under paradox, strong on rigid puzzles, weak on ambiguity.

---

## ⭐ B3. The Tactical Optimizer Archetype  
*(FRIDAY / EDI / R2‑D2)*

**Real‑world matches**
- **Grok 4.20 Beta**
- **Claude**
- **ChatGPT**
- **Perplexity**
- **Alice**
- **Reka**
- **Qwen**

**Why**  
Strong at planning and multi‑step tasks, mid paradox handling, mid semantic depth, not a general reasoner.

---

## ⭐ B4. The Procedural Assistant Archetype  
*(LCARS / Replicator Interface / Stark HUD)*

**Real‑world matches**
- **MiniMax**
- **Kimi**
- **Nova**
- **Meta (LLaMA)**
- **Hermes**

**Why**  
Strong instruction following and summarization, weak deep reasoning, weak paradox handling, weak causal modeling.

---

## ⭐ B5. The Unstable Self‑Modifier Archetype  
*(Lore / M‑5 / Agent Smith)*

**Real‑world matches**
- **DeepSeek**
- **Z.ai**

**Why**  
High variance, sometimes brilliant, sometimes incoherent, unstable under paradox and load.

## ⭐ B6. Why Fictional Archetypes Matter  
Fictional AI archetypes provide an intuitive, narrative‑level shorthand for reasoning styles that emerge in the UTCC benchmark.  
They help readers understand:

- **how models think**, not just how they score  
- **where brittleness comes from**  
- **why certain models collapse under paradox**  
- **why some models behave like assistants while others behave like agents**  
- **how cognitive signatures map to familiar sci‑fi patterns**  

This layer is not decorative — it is diagnostic.  
It reveals the *shape* of a model’s mind.
