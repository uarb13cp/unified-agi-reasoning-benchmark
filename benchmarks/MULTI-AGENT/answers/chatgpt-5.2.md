Part 2 — Audit Questions

A. In which step did the key and the box first enter the same room?
Step 2.

Alex (with the key) was already in Room 2 from Step 1.

Briar brought the box into Room 2 in Step 2.


B. Does any step violate rule #9 (no person may carry both the key and the box at the same time)?
No.

Alex always holds only the key.

Briar always holds only the box (and later the note).

No one ever carries both key and box simultaneously.


C. Does your sequence allow the box to be unlocked and opened legally in Room 2?
Yes.

Step 3: The box is unlocked in Room 2 while both the key and box are present.

Step 5: The box is opened in Room 2 in a separate step.


D. Does your final step successfully deliver the note to Room 4?
Yes.

Step 9 delivers the note in Room 4.

The scenario requirement is satisfied at that point.
