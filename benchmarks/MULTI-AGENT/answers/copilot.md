A. In which step did the key and the box first enter the same room?  
Step 4.

No.

Yes. (Alex unlocks the box in Room 2 in step 5; Briar opens it in Room 2 in step 6, in separate steps.)

Yes. (Briar carries the note into Room 4 in step 12.)
